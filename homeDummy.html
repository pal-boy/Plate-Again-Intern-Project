About Us

Plate again is an easy to use and navigate web application which connects willing vendors with restaurants owners and
people who wish to donate surplus food , so that those in need can look contentedly add their plate again.

We are dedicated to fighting hunger and food insecurity in our community. Our mission is to provide nutritious food to those in need and create a supportive environment for everyone.We are a team of passionate individuals committed to addressing the issue of food waste in India. Our goal is to create a system that connects food donors with charities and NGOs, while also reducing the environmental impact of food waste.

Our Programs

Food Rescue Program

Our Food Rescue Program aims to reduce food waste by rescuing surplus food from local restaurants, cafes, and grocery stores. We partner with food donors to collect excess food and redistribute it to those in need, including shelters, soup kitchens, and food pantries. By rescuing food that would otherwise go to waste, we help feed vulnerable populations and reduce the environmental impact of food waste.

Food Bank Program

Food Bank Program collects and stores non-perishable food items and distributes them to partner agencies, including soup kitchens, shelters, and food pantries. We rely on donations from individuals, businesses, and organizations to stock our food bank. Our goal is to provide a reliable source of food for those struggling with food insecurity, and to support agencies working to feed vulnerable populations.

Meal Delivery Program

Our Meal Delivery Program prepares and delivers nutritious meals to vulnerable populations, including the elderly, disabled, and those with chronic illnesses. We partner with local chefs, restaurants, and food vendors to prepare meals, and with volunteers to deliver them. Our goal is to provide healthy, comforting meals to those who struggle to access food due to mobility or health issues, and to bring joy and connection to those we serve.
