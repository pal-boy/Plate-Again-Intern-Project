Instructions : Before Donate Food

Food waste Do's Food waste Dont's
Check Expiration Dates : Pay attention to expiration dates on perishable items and use the "first in/first out" method to ensure older items are used before newer ones. OverBuy : Avoid buying in bulk unless you know you can use the itmes before they go bad. Large quantities often lead to food waste.
Properly Store Food : store food items according to their specific storage requirements.Keep perishables in the refridgerator,drygoods in airtight containers and use the freezer for items that won't be used soon. Waste Takeout : Be mindful of takeout and restaurants leftovers. If you can't finish a meal,save it for later or share it with someone else.
Use Leftover Creativity : Get creative with leftover by turning them into new meals or freezing them for future use Throwing Away Scraps : Avoid tossing food scraps like vegetable peels,fruit cores and bones in the trash. These can often be used for making broths or composted.
Support Food Banks : Donate non-perishable , unopened food items to local food banks or participate in food drives to help those in need. Cook Too Much : When cooking avoid making ecessive portions that you can't consume or store properly. Start with smaller servings and adjust as needed.
